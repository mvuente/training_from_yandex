There is a tram station. Tram interval A way is a minutes and at B way is b minutes.
Each tram stays within a minute.
Tanya counts trams. She counted n trams on A way and m trams on B way.
What longest and shortest time did Tanya spend on the station?

INPUT:
a
b
n
m

OUTPUT: minTime maxTime

if Tanya made mistake in her counts, OUTPUT is -1
